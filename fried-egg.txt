Ingredients
3 Eggs
1 tea spoon pepper
salt to taste
1 tomatoes( prefereably cut in small pieces)
Butter
Onion to amount


Directions:
Cut onion and tomatoes!
Mix eggs and cutting with pepper.
Add salt and mix!
Put butter in pan and let it melt.
put mixture when butter is melted.
After eggs turn solid, turn it around.

Serve!
